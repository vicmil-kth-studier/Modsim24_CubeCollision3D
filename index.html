2 Mars 2024: The project idea

The basic idea of the project is to implement collisions between cubes in 3D. I would also like to make much of the code reusable for later projects, so I will try to make a library of sorts of help functions and classes. I will call it "vicmil lib" for now.

I am planning to write the project in C++ and use OpenGL coupled with SDL for the graphics part. I will also rely on a library called GLM for some of the linear algebra and maths involved in the project.

    • 12 Mars 2024: Add Collision Physics

    We now move on to implementing the collision part of the simulation. We can start simple and have a cube collide with the ground plane. This builds on our impulse simulation. To resolve the collision requires multiple steps.

    1: determine if the cube is colliding with the ground

    2: separate the cube and the ground(since due to the time steps they might intersect)

    3: calculate the impact position and direction

    4: apply the correct impulse magnitude at the contact
